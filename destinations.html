My Favorite Travel Places

Turkey:


🌊 Istanbul Water (Bosporus Strait)
The Bosporus Strait in Istanbul is a narrow waterway that separates Europe and Asia. It connects the Black Sea and the Sea of Marmara and is one of the busiest sea routes in the world. Ferries, boats, and cruise ships travel here daily, making it both an important transport route and a major tourist attraction. A sunset Bosphorus cruise is one of the most popular experiences in Istanbul. Along the water, you can see palaces, old forts, mosques, and beautiful waterfront houses, showing Istanbul’s rich history and culture.

Pakistan:


🏔️ Kachura Lake (Skardu, Pakistan)
Skardu is home to the beautiful Kachura Lake, which includes Upper and Lower Kachura Lakes. It is known for its crystal-clear blue water, surrounded by snow-covered mountains and green valleys, making it one of the most scenic places in Pakistan. Lower Kachura Lake is also famous for the nearby Shangrila Resort, while Upper Kachura Lake is more natural and less crowded, perfect for peace and hiking. Tourists enjoy boating, photography, and quiet nature views, especially in summer when the weather is pleasant.

Germany:


🏰 Cologne Cathedral (Germany)
Cologne Cathedral (Kölner Dom) is one of the most famous Gothic churches in Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It is located in the city of Cologne, Germany, next to the Rhine River. The cathedral is known for its huge twin towers, which make it one of the tallest churches in the world. Construction started in 1248 but it took more than 600 years to complete. Inside, it has beautiful stained-glass windows, detailed architecture, and religious art. Today, it is one of the most visited landmarks in Germany and a symbol of European history and faith. Visitors also climb the tower for a panoramic view of the city.

United Kingdom:

🕰️ Big Ben (London, UK)
Big Ben is one of the most famous landmarks in the UK, located at the Palace of Westminster in London. “Big Ben” actually refers to the Great Bell inside the tower, while the tower itself is called the Elizabeth Tower. It stands beside the River Thames and is known for its stunning Gothic architecture and the iconic clock faces. The landmark is especially beautiful at night when it is fully lit and is a must-visit spot for tourists in London.
